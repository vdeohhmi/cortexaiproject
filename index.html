About Cortex.ai Bootcamp

Experience an immersive 6-week online program crafted for ambitious high school students. Build real-world AI skills—Python, machine learning, and deep learning—through project-based learning, community collaboration, and expert mentorship.

What You'll Learn

Python & Data Fundamentals

Master coding basics and data handling to prepare for AI workflows.

Machine Learning Foundations

Implement algorithms, evaluate models, and understand feature engineering.

Neural Network Basics

Design and train simple neural networks for classification tasks.

Deep Learning Applications

Work with CNNs and RNNs for image and text data projects.

Capstone Showcase

Collaborate on a real-world AI project and present to peers.

Learn from Certified Educators

All sessions are led by certified high school instructors with real classroom experience, ensuring lessons are engaging, relatable, and aligned with your academic goals.
